Registration

Registration deadline: October 30th

The School has limited places, so applying early is recommended. Applications exceeding the capacity of the School may be placed on a waiting list.

The full fees for ANZSUP are:

  • NZ$960 — includes accommodation at St. Margaret's, all meals and course registration;
  • NZ$360 — includes course registration plus lunches.

Scholarships

Scholarships are available and will cover part of the registration and accommodation package, graduate students from outside Dunedin will be given priority. Applications made prior to July 31st will be considered. Successful applicants will be notified of the outcome via email by 21 August, the remainder of fees needs to be settled by 11 September 2015.

Application

If your application is successful, you will be notified via an acceptance letter and payment details. The fee must be paid within 14 days of notification of acceptance, otherwise the place will be offered to the next applicant on the waiting list.

If you have any registration-related enquiries, please contact Jas Wilson as above.

Visas for international visitors

Visitors from overseas institutions may need to obtain a visa to enter New Zealand. Information regarding the application process can be found here. If you would like additional help with this process or require an official letter of invitation, please contact Jas Wilson (contacts above).

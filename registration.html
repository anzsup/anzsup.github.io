Registration

Registration deadline: October 30th

Registration fee: NZ$200

Scholarship valued at NZ$500 are available for students outside NZ, and preference is made to the applications received earlier. The registration fee includes coffee breaks. Meals are provided at St. Margaret's and the cost is included in the room charge. If you wish to register, please download and fill the registration form, and send it (and/or any other registration-related enquiries) to:

Visas for international visitors

Visitors from overseas institutions may need to obtain a visa to enter Australia. Information regarding the application process can be found here. If you would like additional help with this process or require an official letter of invitation, please contact Jas Wilson (contacts above).

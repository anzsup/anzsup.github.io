School Venue

The fourth School in Ultracold Atomic Physics will be held in Dunedin, New Zealand at the University of Otago hosted by the Dodd-Walls Centre for Photonic and Quantum Technologies.

The workshop will be held in G09 and G400 lecture rooms of the Physics Department on the ground floor of Science III Building. For more details, please see the campus map.

Arrival

By public transport

The only public transport available from the airport is the Shuttle, which can be pre-booked online. The cost is about $20 per person.

By taxi

A taxi from the airport to the University costs about $80–90.

More information is available at the Dunedin Airport website.
